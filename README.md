# CoreX – GigShield AI
### AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Delivery Workers

## Introduction

India’s gig economy relies heavily on delivery partners working for platforms such as Swiggy, Zomato, Amazon, and Zepto. These workers earn income based on completed deliveries.

However, their ability to work is frequently affected by external disruptions such as extreme weather conditions, pollution, and traffic congestion.

GigShield AI provides AI-powered parametric insurance that automatically protects gig workers from income loss caused by such disruptions.

---

## Problem Statement

Delivery partners often experience income loss due to:

- Heavy rainfall preventing deliveries
- Extreme heat reducing outdoor work
- Hazardous air pollution conditions
- Severe traffic congestion
- Local curfews or restricted movement

Existing insurance systems protect vehicles or health but **do not protect income loss**.

GigShield AI addresses this gap.

---

## Target Persona

Food delivery partners working with platforms like Swiggy and Zomato.

Typical characteristics:

- Works 6–10 hours daily
- Earns ₹3000–₹6000 weekly
- Uses smartphone applications

---

## Proposed Solution

GigShield AI is an AI-driven parametric insurance platform that monitors environmental and operational signals affecting delivery work.

These include:

- Weather conditions
- Air pollution levels
- Traffic congestion
- Historical delivery demand

The system calculates an **Income Loss Risk Score**.

If the score crosses a threshold, the platform automatically triggers an insurance payout.

Workers do not need to manually submit claims.

---

## Weekly Premium Model

| Risk Level | Weekly Premium | Coverage |
|------------|---------------|----------|
| Low | ₹40 | ₹1000 |
| Medium | ₹70 | ₹2000 |
| High | ₹100 | ₹4000 |

---

## AI Integration

Machine learning is used for:

- Risk prediction
- Dynamic premium calculation
- Fraud detection

Inputs:

- rainfall
- temperature
- AQI
- traffic congestion
- historical delivery data

Output:

Income Loss Risk Score
